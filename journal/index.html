1. Form Design

2022-01-31

Filling out an online form needs a lot of thought and design behind it. There is a lot of consideration I didn’t know about that was put behind designing forms, more specifically when looking and accessibility, usability, and the number of conversions. Getting a form’s UX to be smooth and encouraging for the user plays a big part in becoming a form that is filled out.

One thing that stood out to me was in practice seven, how the author goes in-depth with talking about labels. Things such as the label position, the proximity and use of space between labels, and even inline labels save us space but prove to create a usability error as they disappear when being typed into
