Article 2: Best Practices for Overlays

2022-01-07

While reading the article, I realized how frequent the use of overlays and modal windows are in interactive media. I like how the article is able to decipher the specific uses of these modal windows, as it gives more clarity for me to better understand how and when to use them. I've come to interact with poorly implemented overlays and thus has resulted in a worse experience or understanding of the website/app being used. The worst use of these is the escape hatch. There are so many times when overlay windows have an escape hatch that is hard to identify or navigate, almost as if the window does want you to escape.

One general piece of advice that I liked from the article was the recommendation to allow users to initiate these modal windows, rather than surprising them as if a form of advertisement.

Give the article a read!
